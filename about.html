About Chirag Pandya

First and foremost, a storyteller, Chirag Pandya, uses his camera to tell stories. With a free and journalistic style, he captures the essence of his subject in a natural and unobstructed way.

Chirag also brings that technical expertise to his work as a portrait travel and wedding photographer, making sure to capture the perfect moment the first time. It also doesn’t hurt that he honed his skills before the digital revolution.

“Over the last 20 years, I have shot travel images for stock photography agencies, magazines, and corporations. Perhaps one of the most rewarding aspects of this job is not the obvious fact of taking pictures of beautiful places and experiencing other cultures. It’s the opportunity to work with amazing people from different agencies, models, producers, stylists, and assistants. Shooting travel images is one of my passions, and I consider myself extremely fortunate to be surrounded by amazing clients.”
